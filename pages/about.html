Our Journey

12 years of growth, innovation, and success

2014 - Foundation

Tech Coderyx founded with a vision to deliver innovative IT solutions. Started with a small team of 5 passionate developers.

2015-2016 - Expansion

Expanded team to 15+ members. Delivered first major enterprise project in education sector. Opened regional offices.

2017-2018 - Diversification

Successfully entered insurance and healthcare domains. Completed 50+ projects. Team grew to 40 members.

2019-2020 - Global Reach

Established presence in 5+ countries. Served clients across 3 continents. Pioneered organic farming tech solutions.

2021-2023 - Growth & Innovation

Reached 100+ team members. Delivered 150+ successful projects. Adopted latest technologies and best practices.

2024-2026 - Market Leader

Recognized as industry leader. Continues innovation in AI/ML, Cloud, and emerging technologies. 50+ global clients.
